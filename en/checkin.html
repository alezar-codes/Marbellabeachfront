3

Entering the Grounds

When you arrive, you should head to Gate 2, just across the street from the Tennis Club:

On the right side of the gate, you will find a keypad. Enter the gate code I provided in your private message.

4

To the Apartment

Come in and turn to the right in few meters:

The small white house on the left is a rubbish storage. Further on the left is your parking lot, No. 28.

Continue straight until you see an elevator. Go to the 2nd floor, and on the right, you will find Apartment 28.

The metal door will be open. Just pull the hook and enter the 6-digit entrance code you received via private message.

5

Parking

On the counter inside the apartment, you will find the remote control for the parking gate:

Return to the parking lot (No. 28). Use the remote to open the gate. Please return it to the apartment before you leave!

6

Common Areas & Exits

For the main gates, exits, and beach access, use the black keypads with the secondary code provided in your message (followed by ENT).
